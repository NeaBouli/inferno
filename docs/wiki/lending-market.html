How It Works

1
Lender creates offer
Deposit IFR on LendingVault. Set how much you want to lend. Earn interest when borrowers repay.
2
Borrower selects offer + posts ETH collateral
Choose an offer, set duration (30–365 days), post 200% ETH collateral. Receive IFR immediately.
3
Repay IFR + interest
Return borrowed IFR plus monthly interest. Get your ETH collateral back. Interest split: 50% lender, 50% protocol.
