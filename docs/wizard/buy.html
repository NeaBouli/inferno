Get $IFR Token

IFR is a deflationary ERC-20 utility token. Follow these steps to get your first tokens during the Bootstrap phase.

Bootstrap is live — contribute ETH directly and claim IFR at a fixed rate before public launch.
  • 1
    Install MetaMaskDownload the MetaMask browser extension or mobile app. Create a new wallet and securely store your seed phrase.
  • 2
    Get ETHPurchase ETH on any exchange (Coinbase, Kraken, Binance) and send it to your MetaMask wallet on Ethereum Mainnet.
  • 3
    Connect & Contribute to BootstrapVisit the Bootstrap page, connect your wallet, and contribute ETH to receive your IFR allocation.
  • 4
    Claim IFRAfter contributing, return to the Bootstrap page to claim your IFR tokens once the phase concludes.
